ChatGPT vs Claude

May 20, 2026Alex Chen10 min read

Two of the most capable AI assistants go head-to-head. Here's how they differ and which one you should use.

Writing & Editing

Winner: Claude. Claude produces cleaner prose, follows tone instructions more reliably, and handles long documents with fewer hallucinations. ChatGPT is strong but sometimes over-explains.

Coding

Winner: Tie. Both excel at Python, JavaScript, and SQL. ChatGPT's ecosystem (Custom GPTs, code interpreter) gives it an edge for data tasks. Claude is better at explaining diffs and reviewing large files.

Reasoning & Math

Winner: ChatGPT on latest models, though Grok leads the category overall. Both handle step-by-step math well with explicit prompting.

Pricing

Both offer free tiers. Paid plans run ~$20/month. API pricing varies — check current rates before building products.

Verdict

  • Choose ChatGPT for versatility and integrations.
  • Choose Claude for writing, editing, and document work.
