Android Tips

May 10, 2026Jordan Lee7 min read

Small settings changes can dramatically improve your Android experience. Here are our top recommendations.

Battery Life

Enable Adaptive Battery, restrict background activity for social apps, and set location to "while using" for most apps. Full guide: Battery tips.

Free Storage

Use Files by Google to find junk and duplicates. Clear cache for heavy apps and move photos to cloud backup. Storage guide.

Privacy

Review permissions quarterly, enable Find My Device, and keep system updates current. Privacy basics.
