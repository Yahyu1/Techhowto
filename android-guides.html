Android Guides

Make your phone faster, safer, and more useful with concise, practical steps for Android 12–15.

Last updated: June 5, 2026By Jordan Lee

Improve Battery Life

  • Adaptive Battery & Brightness: Settings → Battery and display.
  • App-specific restrictions: Limit background activity for heavy apps.
  • Location: Set most apps to "Allow only while using".
  • Use Bedtime mode; disable always-on display if not needed.

Free Storage Safely

  • Files by Google: Clean junk, large files, duplicate photos.
  • Clear app cache for heavy apps (social media, maps).
  • Backup photos to cloud, then remove local copies.

Privacy & Security Basics

  • Enable Find My Device and a strong screen lock.
  • Review app permissions quarterly; revoke unused.
  • Install updates; avoid sideloading from unknown sources.
